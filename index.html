4. Are you licensed and insured to work in Columbus, Ohio?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured in accordance with all Ohio state regulations and Columbus city ordinances. We comply with all relevant building codes and handle all necessary permits and inspections required by the City of Columbus Division of Building & Zoning Services. This ensures your project meets the highest standards of safety, durability, and quality. Working with a properly licensed and insured contractor protects you and your property investment.

5. How long does a typical concrete project take, and what factors affect the timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. A standard residential driveway typically takes 3-5 days from start to finish, while a patio might take 2-4 days. Larger projects like foundations may require more time.

Factors that influence timeline include:

-Size and complexity of the project

-Weather conditions (concrete curing requires appropriate temperatures)

-Site preparation needs (excavation, grading, base material)

-Custom features like stamping or decorative finishes

-Permit processing time

-Concrete curing time (typically 24-48 hours before light foot traffic, 7 days before vehicle traffic)

During your free estimate, we'll provide you with a specific timeline for your project. We pride ourselves on completing work efficiently while never compromising on quality. Our team will keep you informed throughout every phase of your project.
