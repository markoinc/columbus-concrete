About Columbus Concrete & Design Co

Your trusted local concrete contractors serving Columbus and Franklin County

Who We Are

Columbus Concrete & Design Co is a locally owned and operated concrete contracting company proudly serving Columbus, Ohio and the greater Franklin County area. We specialize in residential and commercial concrete services including driveways, patios, slabs, foundations, and decorative stamped concrete.

Our team of experienced concrete professionals is dedicated to delivering quality craftsmanship on every project. We understand that your property is an investment, and we treat every job with the care and attention it deserves.

Our Services

We offer a full range of concrete services to meet your needs:

  • Concrete Driveways - New installations and replacements
  • Concrete Patios - Beautiful outdoor living spaces
  • Concrete Slabs - For sheds, garages, and more
  • Foundations - Solid bases for your structures
  • Stamped & Decorative Concrete - Custom designs and patterns
  • Sidewalks & Walkways - Safe and attractive pathways
  • Concrete Repairs - Restore damaged concrete
